6__doc__=""" 

7This defines additional static data to support CID fonts. 

8 

9Canned data is provided for the Japanese fonts supported by Adobe. We 

10can add Chinese, Korean and Vietnamese in due course. The data was 

11extracted by creating very simple postscript documents and running 

12through Distiller, then examining the resulting PDFs. 

13 

14Each font is described as a big nested dictionary. This lets us keep 

15code out of the module altogether and avoid circular dependencies. 

16 

17The encoding and font data are grouped by some standard 'language 

18prefixes':: 

19 

20 chs = Chinese Simplified (mainland) 

21 cht = Chinese Traditional (Taiwan) 

22 kor = Korean 

23 jpn = Japanese 

24"""
